About this calculator
How it works

Enter your target pace per kilometre using the +/− buttons or by typing directly into the pace fields. The calculator will show you the total finish time and split breakdowns for your chosen race distance.

Split types

  • Even — identical pace for every km
  • Negative — start ~2% slower, finish ~2% faster
  • Positive — start ~2% faster, finish ~2% slower

Estimates

Cadence and stride length are modelled estimates based on your pace. Actual values vary by runner.
